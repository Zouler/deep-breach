# P1.8 — Abyssal Expansion Models

**Phase:** P1.8  
**Branch:** `feature/p1.8-abyssal-expansion-models`  
**Last updated:** 2026-07-06  

---

## Narrative purpose

After **Command Pressure** (P1.7), Research has enough monitoring data to draft competing expansion models. None are conclusive. The player prioritizes one hypothesis for the next analysis cycle — a base-side research briefing, not a dive.

Tone: classified, procedural, restrained uncertainty. No monsters, aliens, or full anomaly explanation.

---

## Unlock condition

P1.8 unlocks when:

- `command_pressure` is in `completedSpineEvents`
- Exactly one P1.7 strategic flag is present:
  - `command_escalation` OR
  - `controlled_observation` OR
  - `emergency_withdrawal_protocols`

Helper: `isAbyssalExpansionModelsPending(state)` in `game/abyssalExpansionModels.ts`

Mission id: `abyssal_expansion_models`

---

## Internal models (briefing content)

| Model | Summary |
|-------|---------|
| **Current Drift** | Footprint behaves like a moving current or pressure disturbance |
| **Resonance Field** | Stationary field causing measurement drift and false-depth behavior |
| **Biological Contamination** | Restrained biological interpretation — particulate cascade, no identified organism |

---

## Player choices (exactly one)

| Choice ID | Label | Story flag | Rewards |
|-----------|-------|------------|---------|
| `prioritize_current_drift` | Prioritize Current Drift Model | `model_current_drift` | +20 Research |
| `prioritize_resonance_field` | Prioritize Resonance Field Model | `model_resonance_field` | +25 Research |
| `prioritize_biological_contamination` | Prioritize Biological Contamination Model | `model_biological_contamination` | +10 Scrap, +10 Research |

Resolution: `resolveAbyssalExpansionModels(state, choice)` or `RESOLVE_ABYSSAL_EXPANSION_MODELS`.

---

## Flags / spine

| Entry | When |
|-------|------|
| `abyssal_expansion_models` (spine) | Once, on any valid model choice |
| `model_current_drift` | Current Drift priority only |
| `model_resonance_field` | Resonance Field priority only |
| `model_biological_contamination` | Biological Contamination priority only |

Mutually exclusive model flags — second resolution is a no-op.

---

## P1.7 strategic response flavor

Briefing and debrief text varies by P1.7 flag via `strategicPostureFlavorForState()`:

| P1.7 flag | Flavor |
|-----------|--------|
| `command_escalation` | Formal Command language; Roberts references external review pressure |
| `controlled_observation` | Science-led procedural tone; balanced department arguments |
| `emergency_withdrawal_protocols` | Engineering defensive; Roberts emphasizes vessel survivability |

Text-only branching — no gameplay systems beyond flags and copy.
